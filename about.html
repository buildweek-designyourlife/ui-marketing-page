Tools for Life Design

Life Design is a Wicked Problem. Wicket Problems are the type of challenge where finding one solution will cause other problems to arise. In choosing a path for your whole entire future, some people think they should just follow their passion, and if it didn't work out- you didn't try hard enough, or you don't truely know yourself. With Design Thinking we will take a more systematic look at your activities and how they effect you.

Our application is based on a class at Stanford, and book called Designing Your Life. The most important principle of designing your life is that you don't really know what you want until you have that experience. We don't want to pursue some goal that we discover we hate once attained. The Life Design App will help you asses what you are doing NOW, and help you measure what activites make you stronger, more focused and energized. This is what we want to do!

Features

Now that you have a working knowledge of Design Thinking and how it can impact the decisions you make, We have created a tool to assist you in this task.

Step one- Take the class from Stanford or you can watch the above video to get the gist.

Step two- use our app to help you record your activities and identify when you are immersed and focused (in flow state). You want at least a week of data.

Step three- At the end of the week, Assess the data and you should see patterns of what activities feel better. Use an AEIOU chriteria.
